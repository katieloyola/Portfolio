About

Katie was born and raised in Salt Lake City, Utah where she developed a love for the mountains and the great outdoors. Growing up, Katie was a competitive figure skater which instilled in her great discipline and a competitive, hard working attitude. After graduating from a small liberal arts high school, Katie made her way to San Diego to take in the big waves and the year-round sunshine California had to offer.

In 2013, Katie graduated from San Diego State University with a B.A. in Social Science and Environmental Studies. Katie has over 10 years of experience working in hospitality, hotels and event management. Her experience includes management roles with the Sundance Film Festival (UT), San Diego Comic-Con International Festival (CA), the Manchester Grand Hyatt (CA), and the Park City Marriott Hotel (UT). After 12 years in San Diego, Katie returned home to Utah and enrolled in the Full-stack Web Development Boot Camp through the University of Utah to take on a new challenge.

Katie currently resides in Salt Lake City, Utah and is working towards becoming a full-time UX/UI designer. When Katie is not behind the screen, you can find her hiking with her dog, Moby, travelling, cooking or river rafting.
